About Centimeters to Meters Conversion

The centimeter (cm) and meter (m) are both units of length in the metric system. The meter is the base unit of length in the International System of Units (SI), while the centimeter is a commonly used subunit.

Conversion Formula: 1 meter = 100 centimeters

To convert centimeters to meters, simply divide the number of centimeters by 100. For example, 250 centimeters equals 2.5 meters (250 รท 100 = 2.5).
